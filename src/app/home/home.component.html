About Us

At Naik AR we are trying to utilize Augmented Reality and 3D modeling in order to enhance online shopping experience for consumers. The developed technology is utilized for virtual fitting of products on ecommerce websites. The web-based nature and its simple integration into existing e-commerce websites makes this technology viable for enhancing the everyday online consumer behavior.

U - Model

U-Model is a web-based platform that enables live fitting of eyewear on e-commerce websites using any device (laptop, tablet and mobile). U-Model gives the experience of fitting and trying on glasses virtually while shopping online.

What?
  • Enables live fitting of eyewear
  • Accessible through the e-commerce website
  • Web based platform
  • Connects directly to the e-commerce website
  • Option of uploading an image to fit on
How?
  • Simple incorporation of up to 8 lines of script in the existing website
  • Platform automatically accessed via a “Try On” button
  • Generates realistic 3D models of eyewear via images provided
  • Want to know more? Connect with us!
Why?
  • To enhance the consumer experience
  • To incorporate innovative technology into online shopping, with minimal effort
  • To decrease the cost of returns due to lack of fitting capabilities
